About Us

South Sudan's Trusted Engineering Partner

Jaweer Engineering & Construction Co. Ltd is a professional engineering and construction company based in Juba, South Sudan, committed to delivering high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective construction and engineering solutions across South Sudan.

The company has built a growing reputation for professionalism, quality workmanship, and strong project execution. With a dedicated team and industry experience, Jaweer Engineering & Construction focuses on delivering projects that meet modern engineering standards while maintaining efficiency, safety, and client satisfaction.

Jaweer Engineering & Construction works closely with clients, partners, and contractors to ensure every project is completed successfully and on schedule. The company continues to expand its presence within the construction sector by contributing to infrastructure and development projects across the region.
